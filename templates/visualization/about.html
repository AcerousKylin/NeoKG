About NeoKG

NeoKG is a Neo4j-based KG visualization demonstration for boosting tech innovation.

Through backend of Django, offering RESTful API and Neo4j Graph DB interaction with Neomodel. With frontend ECharts for dynamic graph visualization.

Developed by AcerousKylin at Tianjin Renai College, I believe in transforming data into art. For inquiries or collaborations, please contact me.
